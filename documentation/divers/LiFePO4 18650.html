Features  

Nominal Voltage   3.2V
Capacity                 
  • Nominal: 1100mAh;
  • Minimum: 1000mAh (1C Discharge)
Charging current
  • Standard: 1.1A, (1C)
  • Maximum: 5.5A, (5C)
Max.Discharging current 49.5A ; 45C (Cell skin temperature can not exceed 85oC)
Operation Temperature
  • Charging: 0 oC (32F) - 45 oC (113F)
  • Discharging: - 20 oC (-4F) - 60 oC (140F)  
Cycle Performance
  • ≥2000 times
Dimensions
  • Length: 65.5±0.3 mm
  • 18.3±0.2 mm
Weight 38.0g±1.0g

Applications

Warnings

  • Don't use NiMH or Li-ion smart charger charge LiFePO4 cell/pack.
  • Wrong type of charger will cause cell to explode. Batteryspace.com will not be responsible for damage
  • LiFePO4 battery must be cut-off at 3.65V/cell when charging
  • You must limit discharging rate below Max rate
  • Please see our damage test report to understand our specs and limit your application within specs
  • Please read more info about LiFePO4 battery
